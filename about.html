Trusted Quality You Can Truly Rely On

Founder Casey Young started Young Guns Roofing with a clear vision — to build a roofing company grounded in transparency, reliability, and craftsmanship. With years of hands-on experience in the industry, he recognised that clients value more than just a finished roof; they value clear communication, dependable timelines, and workmanship built to last.

From day one, the goal has been simple: deliver roofing solutions that homeowners and businesses can feel confident about long after the job is complete.

Trusted Roofing, Expert Leadership

Casey Young is the founder and driving force behind Young Guns Roofing. With extensive hands-on industry experience, he leads the company with a commitment to precision, accountability, and high standards on every project.

Known for his attention to detail and straightforward approach, Casey believes a roof represents more than protection — it represents peace of mind. His leadership has shaped a company culture focused on professionalism, strong work ethic, and consistent quality across both residential and commercial projects.

Under his guidance, Young Guns Roofing has grown through word-of-mouth referrals and repeat clients who appreciate honest advice, transparent pricing, and reliable results.
